# Taiping Rebellion / 太平天国运动 Wiki

This folder is a reader-facing Markdown wiki on the Taiping Rebellion and the Taiping Heavenly Kingdom. It is organized for a person who wants to understand the war as a story first, then explore the sources, debates, people, institutions, and later memory.

**Current state**: 96 markdown files (~183,000 words) across 11 directories. Source integration is underway. On 5 June 2026 the first source-integration pass inventoried 30 sources, mapped 33 claims, translated 9 primary passages, and compiled editorial infrastructure. A second expansion session the same day created 9 new pages and deepened 15 existing pages. Systematic citation upgrading is the next phase of work.

## How to use this wiki

1. **Read the story first**: Start with the main article and the six narrative chapters in `01_Main_Story/`. These give the full chronological arc: Opium War background through the fall of Nanjing and final campaigns.

2. **Drill into topics**: Use the index to find pages on specific leaders, institutions, campaigns, and policies. Cross-links between pages connect people to events and ideas to battles.

3. **Check the reference**: Chronology, glossary, Chinese terms, translated primary passages, and key controversies in `07_Reference/` provide quick lookup and source texts.

4. **Understand the debates**: The thematic essays in `06_Aftermath_and_Memory/` present historiographical controversies — classification debates, the Qing victory question, Jiangnan experience, religious language, and the Xiang/Huai power legacy.

5. **Go to the sources**: `08_Sources_and_Editing/` contains the source guide, bibliography, editorial standards, source database, and the editorial infrastructure files (source inventory, extraction notes, claim map, processing log) for editors.
