# Article Template

# Title / 中文题名

## Lead

Write two to four paragraphs that tell the reader who, what, where, when, and why it matters.

## Terms

List only terms needed for reading the article.

## Narrative

Tell the story in chronological order unless the page is thematic.

## Consequences

Explain what changed because of the event, person, text, or institution.

## Debates

Use plain labels. Do not overfill with academic caution.

## Related pages

Link to pages in this wiki.

## Notes

Use Markdown footnotes.
