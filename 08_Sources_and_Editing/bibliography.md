# Bibliography

## Primary and near-contemporary sources

### Taiping official texts

- 《天朝田亩制度》, Taiping official text. Directly consulted via Wikisource.[^tianmu]
- 洪仁玕, 《资政新篇》, 1859. Directly consulted via Wikisource.[^zizheng]
- 洪秀全, 《原道救世歌》. Directly consulted via Wikisource.[^yuandao]
- 洪秀全, 《原道醒世训》. English translation directly consulted in Michael vol. II-III.[^michael-vol2]
- 何春发, 《英杰归真》/ "Ying jie gui zhen" / Heroes Return to the Truth, Nanjing, 1861. LOC digital record directly consulted [^yingjie]; facsimile on Internet Archive.[^yingjie-facsimile]

### Qing and Chinese records

- 《清实录》, Qing Veritable Records. Portal directly consulted at First Historical Archives.[^fhac]
- 《清政府镇压太平天国档案史料》, 26 volumes, First Historical Archives, 1990-2001.[^qing-archives] (Not digitally available; try CNKI/读秀 or institutional libraries.)
- 金毓黻 et al., 《太平天国史料》, 中华书局, 1955. Publicly archived.[^taiping-shiliao-1955]
- 社科院近代史研究所, 《太平天国资料》(近代史资料专刊), 2013. Publicly archived.[^taiping-ziliao-2013]
- 《太平天国资料》, 科学出版社, 1959. Publicly archived.[^taiping-ziliao-1959]
- 沈云龙 (ed.), 《近代中国史料丛刊》 — 太平天国史料, 文海出版社. Publicly archived.[^shen-yunlong]

### Li Xiucheng's confession

- 罗尔纲 (ed.), 《增补本李秀成自述原稿注》, 中国社会科学出版社, 1995. Publicly archived.[^li-xiu-cheng-critical] — the definitive critical edition.
- 《忠王李秀成自述》. Publicly archived.[^li-xiu-cheng-text]

### Contemporary foreign accounts

- Andrew Wilson, *The Ever-Victorious Army: A History of the Chinese Campaign under Lt. Col. C. G. Gordon*, Blackwood, 1868. Internet Archive: original Google scan [^wilson], best quality Toronto copy.[^wilson-best] Directly consulted.
- Augustus F. Lindley (Lin-Le), *Ti-ping Tien-kwoh: The History of the Ti-ping Revolution*, 2 vols., 1866. Internet Archive (UC Libraries) [^lindley-ia]; Project Gutenberg free ebooks.[^lindley-gutenberg]
- J. M. Callery & M. Yvan, *History of the Insurrection in China*, trans. from French, Harper & Brothers, 1853.[^callery]
- John Scarth, *Twelve Years in China: The People, the Rebels, and the Mandarins*, Thomas Constable, 1860.[^scarth]
- A. E. Hake, *Events in the Taeping Rebellion*, W. H. Allen, 1891 (compiled from Gordon's papers).[^hake]
- Thomas Taylor Meadows, *The Chinese and Their Rebellions*, Smith, Elder, 1856.[^meadows]
- Samuel Mossman, *The Great Taiping Rebellion*, Griffith, Farran, Browne, 1893.[^mossman]
- J. M. Mackie, *Life of Tai-ping-wang, Chief of the Chinese Insurrection*, Dix, Edwards, 1857.[^mackie]

### British diplomatic and parliamentary sources

- *Correspondence, Memorials, Orders in Council and Other Papers Respecting the Taiping Rebellion in China, 1852-64*, British Parliamentary Papers: China, Vol. 32, Irish University Press, 1971. Publicly archived.[^bpp-taiping]
- *British Parliamentary Papers: China*, 42 vols., Irish University Press Area Studies Series, 1971.[^bpp-china]
- *British Parliamentary Papers: Military Affairs in China, 1840-69*.[^bpp-military]

### Treaty-port press

- *North-China Herald*, treaty-port newspaper collection, ~3900 issues (1850-1941). Internet Archive collection.[^north-china-herald] Acquired; keyword indexing not yet completed.[^nch-search]

## Modern scholarship — English language

### Core works (acquired)

- Franz H. Michael, *The Taiping Rebellion: History and Documents*, 3 vols., University of Washington Press, 1966-1971:
  - Vol. I: History (1966). Publicly archived.[^michael-vol1] Directly consulted.
  - Vols. II-III: Documents and Comments (1971). Publicly archived. Contains English translations of ~400 Taiping documents including 天朝田亩制度, 资政新篇, 原道救世歌, 原道醒世训.[^michael-vol2] Directly consulted.
- Philip A. Kuhn, *Rebellion and Its Enemies in Late Imperial China: Militarization and Social Structure, 1796-1864*, Harvard University Press, 1970. Publicly archived.[^kuhn] Directly consulted.
- Thomas H. Reilly, *The Taiping Heavenly Kingdom: Rebellion and the Blasphemy of Empire*, University of Washington Press, 2004. Publicly archived.[^reilly] Directly consulted.
- Tobie Meyer-Fong, *What Remains: Coming to Terms with Civil War in 19th Century China*, Stanford University Press, 2013. Publicly archived.[^meyer] Directly consulted.

### Additional English-language works

- Jonathan Spence, *God's Chinese Son: The Taiping Heavenly Kingdom of Hong Xiuquan*, W. W. Norton, 1996. Internet Archive (borrowable).[^spence] Identified; not yet directly consulted.
- Carl Kilcourse, *Taiping Theology: The Localization of Christianity in China, 1843-64*, Palgrave Macmillan, 2016. Internet Archive (borrowable).[^kilcourse]
- Jian Youwen (简又文), *The Taiping Revolutionary Movement*, Yale University Press, 1973. Internet Archive (borrowable).[^jian]
- W. J. Hail, *Tseng Kuo Fan and the Taiping Rebellion*, Yale University Press, 1927. Internet Archive.[^hail]

## Modern scholarship — Chinese language

### Core works (acquired)

- 罗尔纲, 《太平天国史》 (全4册, 88卷), 中华书局, 1991. Publicly archived.[^luo-github]
- 罗尔纲, 《太平天国史纲》, 商务印书馆, 1937.[^luo-shigang] — the first modern synthesis by the founder of Taiping studies.
- 茅家琦, 《太平天国通史》 (上中下3册), 南京大学出版社, 1991. Publicly archived.[^mao-vol1]
- 罗尔纲全集 (22卷). Publicly archived.[^luo-quanji]
- 刘晨, 《太平天国再研究》, 2025. Publicly archived.[^taiping-zaijiu]
